## LAW 1: The Charlie Kirk Files Forced Disclosure Act -- Law Enforcement

### 1. Budget Reduction Percentage Does Not Match Charter

**Charter states:** "25% automatic budget reduction per month of non-compliance"
**Law 1 actually says:** 10% per month, up to 50% cumulative (Section 7(b))

**What remains:** Law still implements 10% instead of charter's 25%. Either update the law to 25% or update the charter to reflect the intentional reduction to 10%.

---

### 7. Repeated Disclaimers — NOT ADDRESSED

**Charter Attorney Analysis states:** Repeated disclaimer paragraphs undermine legislative credibility.
**Law 1 actual status:** Still contains disclaimers embedded within operative text. Should be consolidated into Section 0 (Congressional Findings) or a preamble.

**What remains:** Move all disclaimers to single location in findings/preamble section.

---

### 8. Standalone Fallback Provision — NOT FULLY ADDRESSED

**Law 1 actual status:** Section 1(g) provides partial fallback for "Designated Trusted Investigator" but no explicit standalone operation clause.

**What remains:** Add explicit: "This Act shall be fully operative regardless of whether any companion legislation is enacted."

---

### 10. Section 12A Naming Discrepancy

**Charter states:** "Prohibition on Classification (Section 12A)"
**Law 1 actual:** Section 12A is titled "Foreign Government Records and Diplomatic Channels," not "Classification Prohibition." Classification prohibition is in Section 4(f).

**What remains:** Either create standalone Section 12A for classification prohibition as charter specifies, or update charter to reflect actual location in Section 4(f).

---

### 11. Israeli IP Surveillance Targeting Not in Schedule A

**Investigation file reveals:** Israeli IP addresses systematically searched for 7 Timpanogos surgeons, medical examiner, defense counsel, crime scene contractor — months before assassination.
**Law 1 Schedule A:** No specific item demanding disclosure of foreign IP targeting of investigation-related personnel.

**What remains:** Add Schedule A item: "All records from NSA, FBI, or any agency documenting foreign government digital surveillance, IP tracking, or electronic monitoring directed at any Schedule B person at any time."

---

### 12. No Autopsy Was Performed -- Law Does Not Address This

**Investigation file reveals:** No autopsy performed despite Utah Code 268-8-205 requiring it. Death certificate signed by hospital doctor.
**Law 1:** Items #167-#171 request autopsy reports but don't address the failure to conduct one or mandate post-hoc forensic examination.

**What remains:** Add Schedule A item demanding: (a) all records explaining why no autopsy was performed, (b) who made the decision, (c) who authorized body's removal, (d) whether post-hoc forensic examination should be mandated.

---

### 13. Corner-Shot Weapon Theory Not in Schedule A

**Law 1 Schedule A:** Items #142-#148 cover ballistics but don't reference Israeli Corner-Shot weapon or 9mm ammunition of Israeli manufacture.

**What remains:** Add Schedule A item requiring ballistic comparisons to non-standard weapons including Israeli Corner-Shot, 9mm Israeli-manufactured ammunition, and concealed-position weapons.
